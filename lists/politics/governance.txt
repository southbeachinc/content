Accountability :: being answerable to someone or something
Checks and balances :: a system in which each branch of government has the power to check or limit the actions of the other branches
Devolution :: the transfer of power from a central government to regional or local governments
Equity :: the quality of being fair and impartial
Federalism :: a system of government in which power is divided between a central government and regional governments
Inclusion :: the quality of being included or welcomed
Participation :: being involved in something
Rule of law :: the principle that all people and institutions are subject to and accountable to law
Separation of powers :: the division of authority between different branches or levels of government
Transparency :: the quality of being able to be seen through
