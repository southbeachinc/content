Theory of Constraints :: A management philosophy that focuses on identifying and removing the bottlenecks that limit an organization's ability to meet its goals.
Bottleneck :: A constraint that limits an organization's ability to meet its goals.
Capacity :: The maximum amount of work that an organization can do in a given period of time.
Efficiency :: The ratio of the output of an organization to the input of resources.
Goal :: An outcome that an organization strives to achieve.
Limit :: A boundary or restriction that an organization sets on its activities.
Objective :: A specific and measurable goal that an organization sets out to achieve.
Productivity :: The ratio of the output of an organization to the input of resources.
Target :: A specific goal that an organization sets out to achieve.
Utilization :: The percentage of an organization's capacity that is being used.
